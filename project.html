AI-Powered Tour Management System

Final Year Project

Problem Statement

Travelers often struggle with planning trips, finding authentic local experiences, and managing bookings across multiple platforms. Traditional travel apps lack personalization and immersive preview capabilities.

Unique Proposition

Our system combines AI-driven recommendations with VR previews to create personalized travel experiences. Users can virtually explore destinations before booking and get AI-curated itineraries based on their preferences.

Technologies Used

  • React.js for frontend
  • C# for backend
  • Python for AI/ML algorithms
  • Unity for VR experiences
  • MySQL for database
  • TensorFlow for recommendation engine

Competitor Information

Unlike standard booking platforms (Booking.com, Expedia), our system focuses on personalized AI recommendations and immersive VR previews, bridging the gap between online research and real-world experience.
